Hotel Facilities

Full modern amenities and facilities are available to guests, and our ensemble of restaurants and bars will satisfy the most discerning taste buds. With a full and comprehensive range of activities lined up for guests, diverse interests and pursuits are matched with top quality facilities and services.

Gym & Fitness

Work up a sweat with state-of-the-art gym facilities and calming views at
Riztonia Fitness Club.

Make full use of the range of training equipment including treadmills,
stationary bikes and elliptical cross-trainers, as well as a Kinesis Technogym pulley wall and free weights, which are perfect for resistance training.

After an invigorating workout, unwind and soothe tired muscles at the hot pool, sauna and steam facilities in the changing rooms.

Pool Area

Visit our stunning pool area, the perfect place to wind down under
the beautiful open sky.Awaken your senses with a swim, cool down after a busy day, or spend a lazy afternoon sipping cocktails under the sun.

Set in an outdoor space, the Swimming Pool is a reflection of leisure and comfort. Our outdoor pool area provides a blissful locale for swimming, relaxing, and socializing.

Games Room

The perfect place to relax on the second floor of our Great House. Tucked in the palms and surrounded by stunning ocean views, this living area is the perfect
place for reading, playing a game or relaxing on the decks.

Relaxation Area

If you're looking for someplace peaceful and quiet, the relaxtion area is the
place to go. The relaxation area is a tranquil oasis within our hotel, designed to provide our guests with a serene and rejuvenating experience.
